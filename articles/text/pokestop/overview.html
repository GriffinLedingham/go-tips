PokeStops

What Are They?

On your Map View, PokéStops are represented by blue squares. When you’re close enough to a PokéStop to search it, the box on top of the blue square will open.

Visit PokéStops to gather Poké Balls and other items. You’ll find PokéStops in the world near public art, unique architecture, or public gathering places.

Items Obtainable From PokeStops

Level 1: PokeBall, Egg

Level 5: Potion, Revive, Raz Berry

Level 10: Super Potion

Level 12: Great Ball

Level 15: Hyper Potion

Level 20: Ultra Ball
